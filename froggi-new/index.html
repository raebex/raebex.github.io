frog·ging
'frôgiNG,fräg-/

noun
1. the act of ripping yarn stitches out.


If you've ever knitted before, I'm pretty sure you've run into the problem of starting over a project because you cast on two extra stitches, or something similarly frustrating.


No more gauging!

The existing gauge system is rather confusing, especially to beginners. Froggi makes sure knitters no longer have to fiddle and waste time with crazy calculations.


Time & sanity saved

Start your project right away and forget about tedious swatching and gauge calucations. Froggi takes away the fear of frogging.


Easy inputs

With Froggi's easy input form, choose variables based on your project, such as yarn weight, needle size, and type of stitch.


Instant calculations

Press the 'calculate!' button and instantly find out the exact number of stitches you need to cast on.


Favorites

Save your results by pressing the heart button, and your projects will be kept in local storage for future reference!
