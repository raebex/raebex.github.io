Tibetan People's Solidarity

China's occupation of Tibet has brought international attention to human rights violations and cultural dismantling since the late 1950s. Half a century later, the Central Tibetan Administration held a conference to begin international protest of Chinese Occupation in Tibet. I designed an invitation and takeaway for the inaugural ceremony. The invitee will recieve a scroll wrapped in a prayer flag, which will be the RSVP method for the event. At the end of the ceremony, attendees will string together all the prayer flags and bless the Tibetan people for their freedom.
